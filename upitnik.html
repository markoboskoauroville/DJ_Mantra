Upitnik za DJ nastupDJ booking questionnaire

Kako bih pripremio pravi set i točnu ponudu, molim vas odgovorite na pitanja ispod. Traje otprilike 5 minuta.
To prepare the right set and an accurate quote, please answer the questions below. Takes about 5 minutes.

Slavlje / The event
Name of the birthday person and the guests' age range
Date and time of the performance
Lijevo: početak / Right: end
Any specific theme or mood for the party
Lokacija / The location
City or place and exact location (villa, beach, terrace)
Link to photos or a map of the venue
Venue type
Is there power access near the DJ area?
Gosti / The crowd
Number of guests
Who is attending?
Character of the party
Glazba / The music
Genres or artists the birthday person and guests enjoy
Energy curve for the night
Songs or genres specifically wanted, or to avoid
Tehnika i logistika / Technical & logistics
Sound system and lighting
If I bring gear, how does transport to the venue work?
Length of the performance (hours)
Do they cover travel and accommodation if an overnight stay is needed?
Kontakt / Contact
Name and contact of the person booking
Sažetak / Summary
E-mail WhatsApp

Pošaljite odgovore Marku putem WhatsAppa ili e-maila, ili kopirajte tekst.
Send the answers to Marko via WhatsApp or email, or copy the text.
